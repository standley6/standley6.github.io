Medical Field: Pediatric Surgeon Information

Job Description

Diagnose and perform surgery to treat fetal abnormalities and birth defects, diseases, and injuries in fetuses, premature and newborn infants, children, and adolescents. Includes all pediatric surgical specialties and subspecialties.

Employers

  • Loyola University Medical System
  • Cook County Health System
  • Edward-Elmhurst Health

Related Jobs

There are plenty of related jobs out there, but these three jobs that I found could be the most appealing to you. A surgical technologist ssists with surgeries that doctors perform. They set up the operating theatre, arrange the equipment, and assist the main doctor in operations. A vascular surgeon diagnoses and care for patients who have diseases in the arteries, veins, and lymphatic system. An orthopedic surgeon diagnoses and treats patients with problems in their muscular and sketetal systems.


Skills Required

A pediatric surgeon requires extensive knowledge of pediatric pharmacology and physiology, excellent precision in surgeries, and stong leadership and decision-making skills.

Education Required

For all who want to become pediatric surgeon, you need at to have a 4-year bachelor degree and go through a 4-year medical program.

Experience Required

After passing the bachelor program and the medical program, a student needs to complete a general surgery residency for 2-5 years and complete a pediatric surgery residency for 1-2 years.
